About

Almost 30 Years of Data!

As America's 3rd most popular sport, it's no wonder basketball stats and data is so highly sought after. Our site provides clean data in handy .csv files that will provide you information on NBA players, teams, seasons statistics, and win and losses percentages from 1979 to 2018. This data can be used to provide answers to some of your NBA research questions.
