About

Almost 30 Years of Data!

As America's 3rd most popular sport, it's no wonder basketball stats and data is so highly sought after. Our site provides clean data in handy .csv files that will provide you information on NBA players, teams, seasons statistics, and win and losses percentages from 1979 to 2018. This data can be used to provide answers to some of your NBA research questions.

Your Questions Answered!

Possible data uses/questions:

  • Do teams with “same-state competitors” perform better or worse compared to those that are the only team in a state?
  • Is there a trend regarding championship wins?
  • Do these teams have better players?
  • Most successful team per decade
  • Most NBA titles in year range
  • Scoring leader in year range
  • Does average age of players correlate to team wins?

Reliable Sources Only!

We pulled our information from relevant resources:

  • Team Rankings: Win Trends
  • LOB: Teams by Year
  • Sports Data: Assorted NBA data
  • Kaggle: Player Stats
